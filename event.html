clutural event:


Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of art, music, dance, and drama at our annual Cultural Fest! This event celebrates the diverse talents and creativity of students, providing a platform for cultural expression and collaboration. From energetic dance performances and soulful musical renditions to theatrical plays and captivating art exhibitions, the fest is a blend of tradition and modernity. With interactive workshops, food stalls, and competitions, the Cultural Fest promises an unforgettable experience for participants and spectators alike. Join us as we celebrate the spirit of creativity and cultural heritage!

Go_kart challange


Feel the adrenaline rush and witness the marvels of engineering in action at our thrilling Go-Kart Challenge! This event brings together aspiring automotive enthusiasts and engineers to design, build, and race their own go-karts. Teams from various institutions will battle it out on the track, showcasing innovation, teamwork, and technical expertise. The event features multiple rounds, including design evaluation, endurance runs, and a high-octane race to the finish. Whether you're a motorsport fan or a curious spectator, the Go-Kart Challenge is sure to leave you on the edge of your seat.

Hackathon


Calling all tech enthusiasts and problem solvers! Our Hackathon is the ultimate stage for innovators to showcase their skills and creativity. Over 24 hours of intense coding, brainstorming, and collaboration, participants will develop solutions to real-world challenges. With themes ranging from AI and sustainability to healthcare and education, this event offers a platform for tech-savvy individuals to create impactful projects. Guided by industry experts and judged by a distinguished panel, the Hackathon is not just a competition but a celebration of innovation, teamwork, and the power of technology to make a difference.
